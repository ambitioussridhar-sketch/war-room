Stakeholder strategy simulator v3.0

The War Room

Every idea at work has to survive the people around it. Model your stakeholders as players, test your move against them, and see the objections and your best counter before the real meeting. Runs entirely in your browser.

How it works

  1. Add the people around your idea to The Board — pick their personality traits, their stance toward you, and what they gain or lose if you succeed.
  2. Describe the move you're about to make: a proposal, an email, a pitch.
  3. Run the simulation. The engine plays each person in character and shows their private read, likely move, objections, and your best counter.

Downloads an AI model (~0.9–1.1GB) to your browser (one time). Runs entirely offline after that. Requires a modern browser with WebGPU support (Chrome 113+, Edge 113+). Auto-detects your GPU capability for best compatibility.
